PRIMARY INGREDIENTS

Specially formulated fish nutrition with a balanced blend of high quality bioactive ingredients, essential vitamins and minerals designed to keep your fish at their prime, Always.


Contents 
100 ml (100 g / 0.8 oz.)


Feeding recommendation
Feed twice every alternate day, only as much as the fish take up within 10 minutes.


Ingredients
Fish Protein, Krill, Probiotics - Llactobacillus spp, bifidobacterium spp, Streptococcus spp, Bacillus Spp, Amaranth Flour, Soy Flour, Pea Flour, Fish-extarcted Lipids, Pepsin, Amino Acids, Brewers Yeast, Ca-caseinate, Gammarus, Whole Egg Powder, Mannan Oligosaccharides (0.4%), Cod-liver Oil (Containing 34% Omega Fatty Acids), Spirulina (8%), Fupa, Natural Flaxseed Extract, Herbs, Turmeric, Alfalfa, Carrots, Whole Egg, Pisum Sativum, Kale Extract, Stinging Nettle, Green-lipped Mussel Meal, Sea Algae, Parsley, Paprika, Natural Carotenoids, Spinach, Garlic.
